ChinaTeach is an American based, full-service English teacher recruiter. We provide teachers with great teaching jobs throughout Beijing and are here to help before and after your arrival in China. The majority of our teachers are placed in Chinese kindergartens and English training centers, but we also have partnerships with high schools and universitiesPlease tell us of your preference and we will work to accommodate your needs. To qualify for one of our teaching positions, you must be energetic, patient, and love working with children. We prefer candidates who possess a bachelor’s degree or have considerable teaching experience.

We provide comprehensive compensation packages that may include:

1. Competitive salaries - ALL Contract Options are fully translated into English and provide English Teachers with a very comfortable standard of living. Our teachers have very strong purchasing power and are paid well above the average Chinese persons salary.
2. Round-trip airfare reimbursement
3. Free accommodation -- Our English Teachers have the option to live in our quality housing in great locations very close to their teaching location. All of our apartments come equipped with all of the western style amenities that you'd expect and need.
4. Visa Sponsorship​
5. Free Chinese lessons​
6. Free Lunch at school
7. Six, Ten or Twelve month contracts.

We make your transition to China very easy. You will be picked up from the airport upon your arrival in China and you will receive temporary housing with many other new teachers where you'll go through an orientation on life in China. You will also receive a crash-course in TEFL, living in China and be instructed in the the best ways to approach your new job. Once placed in a school, you will move into to your new apartment and be provided with a Chinese Assistant. Our Assistants are here to help you with the many different situations that will arise when living in an unfamiliar country and to alleviate some of the culture shock that comes with living in China. Examples include introducing teachers to the staff of their new schools and showing the route to their school. They'll help you open a bank account and complete your police registration (In Beijing all residents have to register their address with the police department). There is definitely a language barrier that exists between Chinese people and expats but you will have help with translation services both inside and outside of your school. As a person coming to China for the first time, we really do our best to make the transition as painless for you as possible and alleviate some of the culture shock that comes with living in an unfamiliar land. In any situation where a teacher is reasonably in need, you will have a strong support system to lean on. What sets BeijingTeach apart from nearly every other recruiting service you'll encounter is that we genuinely care about our teachers. Our staff consists of Americans and Chinese nationals who work day and night to make sure that you are enjoying your time in the capital city of China. We are happy to put you in contact with former and current teachers who are more than willing to share their experiences with you.​​​​
